Time (Saa)

In this section, you will learn about day, months, time and seasons.

Time - Saa

In english, time is counted from midnight so 1am is the first hour of the day. However, in Ma'di ti, time is counted from sunrise

Day

In Ma'di ti, Monday - Saturday is expressed as 'day 1', 'day 2', day 3' etc. for example, Monday is 'Itu alu', the word alu meaning one.

However the word for Sunday is 'itu rubanga', Rubanga means 'God' so essentially 'itu rubanga' is translated to 'God's day'

English Ma'di
Monday Itu alu
Tuesday Itu eri
Wednesday Itu na
Thursday Itu su
Friday Itu tou
Saturday Itu azia
Sunday Itu rubanga

Month - Imba

In ma'di ti months is expressed as 'month 1', 'month 2', 'month 3' etc. The word for month is 'imba' so 'imba alu' is January.

English Ma'di
January Imba alu
Febuary Imba eri
March Imba na
April Imba su
May Imba tou
June Imba azia
July Imba tuderi
August Imba aro
September Imba dricalu
October Imba mudri
November Imba mudri limbi alu
December Imba mudri limbi eri
